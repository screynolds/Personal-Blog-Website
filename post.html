Hot Cocoa Around the World

How this cozy winter drink is prepared across four different cultures.

February 28th, 2021

Mexico - Champurrado

Prepared using Mexican chocolate tablets and masa de maiz or masa harina, which is the flour used to make tortillas. It's mixed with spices like cinnamon and star annise and served with a churro!

Italy - Cioccolata Calda

Italian hot chocolate is incredibly thick and uses cornstarch to achieve its creamy texture. This pudding-like drink pairs well with a panettone.

Belgium - Belgian Hot Chocolate

Belgium is known worldwide for its decadent chocolate! Like the Italian variation, Belgian hot chocolate is thick and creamy. The most famous recipe from Brussels uses both bittersweet and semisweet, as well as dark and light chocolate. Seasoned with a pinch of salt.

India - Chai Hot Chocolate

The Indian variation made with black tea includes an aromatic blend of chai spices, mixing ginger, cinnamon, and cardamom.
